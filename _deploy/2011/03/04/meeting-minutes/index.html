04 Mar 2011


As an overview of what we will be doing:

  • Our plan is to set up a booth in the IACC, provided we are allowed, where we will take shifts playing the games.
  • Naturally, the game will be very boring or very bad, we must choose which ones we will choose.
  • We are doing this to raise awareness of the charity "Child's Play" and to try to get donations for it.

Discussion:

  • What will the format and structure of this thing be?
    • We will play some game for an extended period of time.
    • It will take place sometime after spring break, maybe in early April.
    • For corporate sponsors, Austen and Nathan will be in charge of contacting corporate sponsors.
  • We should probably make sure that we aren't extremely loud in the IACC because regular classes will be going on during this time.
  • Game Possibilities???
    • Desert Bus
    • Tetris
    • Dragon Warrior
  • We should probably have n+1 people at any given time at the booth, where n is the number of people playing a game.
  • Austen will talk to ITS next week about taking up space for this in the IACC.
  • We will have a leader board displayed that will keep track of the hourly totals put in from each ACM member.

Decisions:

  • Game: Farming Simulator 2011
  • Duration: Start on a Monday in early April, and go until at most Sunday.
  • Adam will buy the game this weekend and find out the mechanics of the game.
  • Decorations such as the member being dressed up and sit in an old bucket seat type chair.

We will plan to meet next week, Tuesday at 7pm to discuss a little more about the details.
